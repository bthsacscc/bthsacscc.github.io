About Us

We proudly present to you ...

We aim to help students who are passionate about the sciences, to introduce career choices in chemistry, and most importantly: to share our love of chemistry.

1. Guest Speakers

We will have guest speakers from ACS (American Chemistry Society) who will provide insight about science-related careers.

2. Tutoring Program

Our club offers a tutoring program for those who need help with their chemistry classes. We will be able to help those currently in a Regents Chemistry or AP Chemistry class.

3. Newsletter

We are introducing a newsletter will be written by the executives, and our lovely club members! Our newsletter is our method of changing chemistry into something that is fun and simple to learn.

4. Projects

Finally, we will have projects for you to do at home, which we will share on our gallery page and instagram!

5. COVID-19 Impact

Once everyone is back to in-person learning, we will be able to perform fun chemistry experiments at school, and offer commmunity service hours to our members.
